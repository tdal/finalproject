The idea

I have always been a very active sleeper and an occasional sleepwalker. People have been telling me that snore and talk in my sleep all my life. That's why I want to track my sleep patterns and get to the bottom of this: Am I really a heavy snorer? What influences my behavior during sleep? And what do the things that I dream tell about me?

The data

For one week, I tracked my sleeping behavior with a FitBit wristband and an App called SnoreLab which records snoring and analyzes the sound files.

The conclusion

Looking at the data, I have got to say: My sleeping problem is not as bad as I thought. The times at night that I spend restless or awake are absolutely average. And yes, I am definitely a snorer, but my snoring is not as horrible as some people made it seem (trust me, I have listened to the recordings). Now, I want to take a deeper look at the data and find out if there are any changing patterns in my sleeping behavior and if there are any external factors influencing it. Also, I want to analyze my dreams and find ways to visualize what is happening in them.
